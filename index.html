About us

Before the war, we lived in the big beautiful city of Dnepr in the eastern part of Ukraine. Our family is small and friendly. We have been together for 9 years, our dog joined us 7 years ago. We love to travel, we have been to different European countries: Sweden, Slovakia, Poland, France, Turkey, Estonia, Finland and others. A few years ago we traveled in Portugal. And this spring we were planning a trip to Iceland to see the whales. On February 24, 2022, our plan was changed, our goal is simply to survive.
